Task Grinder

What to expect

With this web app you can manage your tasks and give them a quest like character. You can assign a difficulty to your tasks based on the motivation you need to complete them. Earn xp by completing tasks in time to level up. As a motivation you can define rewards for leveling up. A reward may be a nice Diner or watching a movie in cinema. This should help you building a routine doing daily tasks that feels rewarding.
